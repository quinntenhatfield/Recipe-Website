Chicken Tikka Masala

⏱️ Cook Time: 40 mins

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 can (400g) tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, and paprika for 1 hour.
  2. Grill or pan-cook chicken pieces until slightly charred. Set aside.
  3. In a pan, melt butter and sauté onions until translucent.
  4. Add garlic, ginger, turmeric, garam masala, and chili powder. Cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add tomato puree and simmer for 10 minutes. Stir in cream.
  6. Add chicken and cook for 10 more minutes. Garnish with cilantro and serve with rice or naan.

History

Chicken Tikka Masala is a popular Indo-British dish said to have originated in the UK by South Asian chefs adapting Indian flavors. Its creamy, spiced tomato sauce has made it a worldwide favorite and a symbol of British multicultural cuisine.
