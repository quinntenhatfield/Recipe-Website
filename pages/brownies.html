Brownies

⏱️ Cook Time: 35 mins

Ingredients

  • 10 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocao powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F and line 8"x8" baking pan with parchment paper..
  2. Combine butter and sugar in bowl and microwave for 30 seconds. Mix with whist. Repeart 5 times total, until glossy.
  3. Add eggs and which thouroughly until light in color then add vanilla extract.
  4. Add cocao powder, flour, salt, and mix. Then add chocolate chips.
  5. Bake for 25 minutes.

History

Brownies originated in the United States in the late 1800's when a baker didn't add baking powder to their chocolate cake batter which resulted in a dense fudgy "cake".
