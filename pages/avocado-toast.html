Avocado Toast

⏱️ Cook Time: 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, poached egg, chili flakes

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden and crispy.
  2. Mash the avocado in a bowl with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread mashed avocado on the toasted bread.
  4. Add your favorite toppings like tomatoes, feta, or a poached egg.
  5. Serve immediately while warm and crunchy.

History

Avocado toast rose to fame in the 2010s as a symbol of health-conscious and minimalist dining. Though avocados have been eaten for centuries, their combination with toast became a brunch staple thanks to cafés in Australia and California embracing the trend.
