Spaghetti Carbonara

⏱️ Cook Time: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Fry pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. Beat eggs and mix with grated parmesan.
  4. Drain spaghetti and mix with pancetta.
  5. Remove from heat, then stir in egg mixture.
  6. Season with black pepper and serve.

History

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish believed to have originated in Rome during the mid-20th century. Traditionally made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper, it reflects the simplicity and bold flavors of Roman cuisine. Some theories even link its name to coal miners, or "carbonari," who enjoyed the hearty dish.
