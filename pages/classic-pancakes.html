Classic Pancakes

⏱️ Cook Time: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp butter, melted

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using about 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Brown on both sides and serve hot with syrup, butter, or fruit.

History

Pancakes have been a breakfast favorite around the world for centuries. Early versions date back to ancient Greece and Rome. The modern American pancake, fluffy and golden, became popular in the 19th century and has remained a beloved staple ever since.
