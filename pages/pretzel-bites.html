Pretzel Bites

⏱️ Cook Time: 60 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 .25 oz pack active dry yeast
  • 4 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 9 cups regular temperature water
  • 1/3 cup baking soda
  • 1 large egg

Instructions

  1. Whist together the warm water and sugar in a large bowl, then dust with the yeast and wait 5 minutes for the yeast to foam and activate.
  2. Add flour, melted butter, and salt and mix until completely combined. Then use hands to knead dough for roughly 4 minutes until smooth.
  3. Move dough to lightly oiled bowl and let sit for 20-30 minutes in a warm place until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat oven to 450 F. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  5. Seperately, combine water and baking soda and bring to a boil over mid-high heat.
  6. Divide dough into 12 equal pieces and roll out into 3/4 inch wide ropes. Cut rope into individual sized bites of your choosing.
  7. Boil the pretzel bites for about 30 seconds then place on parchment paper sheets such that theyre not touching.
  8. Brush each pretzel bite with egg wash and sprinkle sea salt over top.
  9. Bake for 12-14 minutes until browned.

History

Pretzels are believed to originate in the 6th century Europe, created by monks who twisted bread to represent prayer. Over time it spread worldwide, in some cultures pretzels are believed to represent good luck.
