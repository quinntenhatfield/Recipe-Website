Chicken Alfredo

⏱️ Cook Time: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 200g fettuccine
  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper
  • Parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook chicken with salt and pepper until golden.
  3. Remove chicken, then melt butter and sauté garlic.
  4. Add heavy cream and parmesan, stirring until smooth.
  5. Return chicken to pan and mix with sauce.
  6. Toss with pasta, garnish with parsley, and serve.

History

Chicken Alfredo has its roots in early 20th-century Italy, created by Alfredo di Lelio in Rome. Originally a simple fettuccine dish with butter and Parmesan, it was later Americanized by adding cream and chicken, becoming the rich, creamy pasta favorite known today.
