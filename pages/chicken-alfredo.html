Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 200 fettuccine
  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper
  • Parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook chicken with salt and pepper until golden.
  3. Remove chicken, then melt butter and sauté garlic.
  4. Add heavy cream and parmesan, stirring until smooth.
  5. Return chicken to pan and mix with sauce.
  6. Toss with pasta, garnish with parsley, and serve.
