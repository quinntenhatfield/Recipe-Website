Dill Pickles

⏱️ Cook Time: 48 Hours

Ingredients

  • 12 kirby cucumbers
  • 3 minced garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp mustard seeds
  • 1 sprig fresh dill
  • 1 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 2 tbsp sugar

Instructions

  1. Pack cucumbers into heat-safe glass jar.
  2. Add garlic, mustard seeds, and dill to the jar.
  3. In a seperate pot mix the vinegar, water, salt, and sugar and bring to a boil.
  4. Pur the brine into the container with the cucumbers and seal for a minimun of 48 hours.

History

Pickles date back over 4,000 years to ancient Mesopotamia where cucumbers were preserved to extend their shelf life. Over time "pickling" has spread across the globe to prepare and preserve vegetables, and pickles themselves have become a staple of worldwide cuisine.
