Chocolate Chip Cookies

⏱️ Cook Time: 25 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup salted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar, packed
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees F and line 3 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a seperate bowl creame together butter and sugars until combined.
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla to butter mixture until lightly colored.
  5. Mix dry and wet ingredients together.
  6. Add chocolate chips to mixture.
  7. Roll 2-3 tbsp balls of dough.
  8. Bake in oven for 8-10 minutes, to prefered softness.
  9. Let sit on baking pan for 5 minutes after removal from oven.

History

Chocolate Chip Cookies were an acccidental creation after choclate chips were added to a batch of Butter Drop Do cookies as a sweet substitute and unexpededly held their shape. The recipe was massively popular during WWII and soon adopted by Nestle.
