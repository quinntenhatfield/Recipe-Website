Salsa

⏱️ Cook Time: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 jalapeno
  • 1 serrano pepper
  • 1 can of diced tomatos
  • 4 peeled tomatoes
  • 1/3 large red onion
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp white distilled vinegar
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add broccoli, carrots, and bell pepper, stir-fry for 3 minutes.
  4. Add zucchini and soy sauce, cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve hot.

History

Stir-frying is a traditional Chinese cooking technique that dates back over 1,500 years. Quick, high-heat cooking preserves texture and nutrients, making stir-fry a staple across Asia. The vegetable stir fry we know today blends these roots with global flavors for a healthy, fast, and versatile dish.
